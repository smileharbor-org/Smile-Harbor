Overview

Smile harbor is a youth-led organization that aims to foster and facilitate all forms of education, with a goal to lay special emphasis on the holistic development of the individual along with awakening a social consciousness in citizens. The organiztion opeates across a network of different schools and organizations, giving a platform to all the students to follow their passion and create it into something meaningful even turn into a viable career choice.

Education

Our target is to get every child their minimum education to be a good citizen of this country and we focus on to follow their passion.All primary age children are attending school but your help is needed to ensure that the children all continue going to school.

Medical & Nutrition

Enabling young children to achieve their full developmental potential is a human right an essential requisite for sustainable development. Among many sectors, health sector has an important role & responsibility to support nuturing care for every childhood development.

The Mission

The Mission

Phasellus auctor felis quis
risus varius ac posuere massa dapibus.

1. Eradicate extreme poverty and hunger

  • Reduce by half the proportion of people living on less than a dollar a day.
  • Reduce by half the proportion of people who suffer from hunger.

2. Achieve universal primary education

  • Ensure that all boys and girls complete a full course of primary schooling.

3. Promote gender equality and empower women

  • Eliminate gender disparity in primary and secondary education preferably by 2005, and at all levels by 2015.

4. Reduce child mortality

  • Reduce by two thirds the mortality rate among children under five.

5. Ensure environmental sustainability

  • Integrate the principles of sustainable development into country policies and programs; reverse loss of environmental resources.
  • Reduce by half the proportion of people without sustainable access to safe drinking water and basic sanitation.
  • Achieve significant improvement in lives of at least 100 million slum dwellers, by 2020.

6. Develop a global partnership for development

  • Develop further an open, rule-based, predictable, non-discriminatory trading and financial system.
  • Address the special needs of the least developed countries, landlocked countries and small island developing States.
